Spring Desserts

1. Lemon Blueberry Cinnamon Rolls

Embrace the freshness of spring with these citrusy lemon blueberry cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • For the filling: 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, 1/4 cup softened butter

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it stand for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Add milk, melted butter, lemon zest, and yeast mixture. Mix until dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the dough and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle fresh blueberries over the top.
  5. Roll up the dough tightly and cut into slices. Place in a greased pan and let them rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake at 350째F (175째C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your lemon blueberry cinnamon rolls!

2. Strawberry Shortcake Muffins

Enjoy the sweetness of spring with these delightful strawberry shortcake-inspired muffins.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, diced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C) and line muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, combine milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in diced strawberries.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full and bake for 18-20 minutes.
  6. Let the muffins cool before serving.
