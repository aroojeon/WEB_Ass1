Winter Desserts

1. Warm Cinnamon Rolls

Delight in the aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls, perfect for a cozy winter morning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the filling: 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, 1/4 cup softened butter

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it stand for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Add milk, melted butter, vanilla extract, and yeast mixture. Mix until dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the dough and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Roll up the dough tightly and cut into slices. Place in a greased pan and let them rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake at 350째F (175째C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your warm cinnamon rolls!

2. Chocolate Muffins

Indulge in these moist and chocolaty muffins, a delightful treat for the winter season.

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C) and line muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full and bake for 18-20 minutes.
  6. Let the muffins cool before serving.
