Fall Desserts

1. Pumpkin Spice Cinnamon Rolls

Enjoy the warm flavors of fall with these pumpkin spice-infused cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • For the filling: 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, 1/4 cup softened butter

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it stand for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Add pumpkin puree, milk, melted butter, and yeast mixture. Mix until dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the dough and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Roll up the dough tightly and cut into slices. Place in a greased pan and let them rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake at 350째F (175째C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your pumpkin spice cinnamon rolls!

2. Apple Streusel Muffins

Deliciously spiced muffins with chunks of fresh apples and a buttery streusel topping.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups peeled, cored, and chopped apples

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C) and line muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In another bowl, combine milk, oil, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in chopped apples.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together streusel topping: 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and 2 tablespoons melted butter. Sprinkle over muffins.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Enjoy your apple streusel muffins!
